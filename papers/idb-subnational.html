Subnational fiscal data for Latin America

As a data-science consultant to the Inter-American Development Bank's Fiscal Management Division, I worked on subnational public finance across ten Latin American countries. I homogenised and processed the underlying fiscal data, built the interactive maps below, and co-authored the written outputs that came from it — a sector framework on decentralisation and a study of the property tax in Mexico.
